How to Play

Goal: Select the tile with the highest number.

Rules:

  • Click any tile to reveal its value.
  • You must then Accept it (which ends the game) or Reject it (and move on).
  • You can never go back to a rejected tile!

Hint: Picking your first tile gives you a 1/30 (3.3%) chance to win. There is a strategy that *massively* increases your odds. Can you discover it?
